The complete, canonical list of all episodes from the Prologue through The Breaking Point.

Lost/Thread Series Premiere (Prologue + Ep. 1)

A tight-knit group of college friends sets out to document the unexplained, following cryptic online posts that hint at hidden truths. Their curiosity drives them to perform a ritual—one they believe will open the door to something bigger. What starts as a calculated experiment quickly spirals into something far more dangerous.

Prologue: The First Message

John promised he was done chasing the unexplainable. But when a cryptic email from an old friend arrives, attached with recordings too strange to ignore, he finds himself drawn back into the mystery that once consumed his life.

Episode 1: Outsider

Rue Asfaw reunites with her childhood friend James, expecting a quiet visit. Instead, she finds herself surrounded by strangers, all preparing for a trip no one will fully explain. Tensions surface quickly as Rue senses something deeper beneath the surface, and James isn’t telling her everything.

Episode 2: Crossing Over

The group sets out to perform the ritual. At first conversation is light and casual, but just before everything starts, James gets serious.

Episode 3: The Space Between

The ritual worked… but no one knows what that means. Still dazed, the group tries to pull themselves together as the weight of what they’ve started begins to settle in. Rue is the most shaken.

Episode 4: The Things We Hear

The group wakes to quiet calm, trying to convince themselves things feel normal. But Rue knows better. Something changed last night. The fog gets closer, and something starts dragging itself through the trees, watching… waiting.

Episode 5: Precious Little Things

Stranded and out of gas, the group encounters a mysterious gas station and its cryptic clerk. They soon learn that getting back on the road might require more than just fuel, and the journey ahead is far more dangerous than they imagined.

Episode 6: Shadows in the Fire

A reckless act of defiance at the gas station unleashes a terrifying, unseen predator that stalks the group. The entity invades their minds, using their own voices and deepest fears as weapons, resulting in a devastating encounter that will cost the group dearly.

Episode 7: Night Special - The Weight of Knowing

The late shift at a diner is usually quiet, but for Priya, something feels off. When a mysterious woman arrives, Priya is left with a chilling realization that the world is not as solid as it seems.

Episode 8: The Path We Burn

Stranded and mourning a devastating loss, the shared trauma tears the group apart. As they try to salvage what's left, fear, grief, and anger finally boil over, pushing one of them to their breaking point.

Episode 9: Fiver Pitch

Exhausted, the team pushes on through a dangerous storm, forcing them to take shelter at the Second Well Inn—a place that feels warm and welcoming, but comes with a very specific set of rules.

Episode 10: The Breaking Point

Supplies are low and Malik is fading, pushing the group’s tension to a breaking point. Rue wakes to a disorienting new reality where the rules of existence have changed, isolating her in a shifting nightmare.
